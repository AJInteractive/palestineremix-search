SUHAIL SKAIK GAZA POWER STATION DIRECTOR The people of Gaza have learnt the timetable of power cuts Sometimes the outage lasts for eight hours in the day Other times it lasts eight hours during the night The third day there is no power cut When it is cold people use electric heaters as the houses are poorly insulated More electricity is consumed though our power resources are the same Demand increases and so do the duration of power cuts That's why the timetables keep changing
