FADI QATTAN The 2001 invasion of Bethlehem was probably one of the toughest times for the city Occupation forces invaded imposed a curfew and sieged the Church of the Nativity with people hiding inside The siege was humiliating for such a sacred place They fired live ammunition at the Church and priests trapped inside had their rooms shot at and windows broken The church bell ringer was up the tower trying to ring the bells when an Israeli sniper killed him making him a martyr
