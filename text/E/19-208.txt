Prof Avi Shlaim Glubb is a very complex character He was not the simple minded soldier that he pretended to be but he was a highly sophisticated politician who imposed on the Arabs Britain's partition plan There was a meeting between Ernest Bevin the Labour government's foreign secretary and Tawfik Abu al Huda the Jordanian prime minister who was accompanied by Glubb Pasha who also acted as an interpreter Bevin asked Abu Al Huda 'what do you plan to do?' and Abu Al Huda said 'we plan to send the Arab Legion to protect and keep the Arab part of Palestine' and Bevin said 'that seems the sensible thing to do but don't go and invade the Jewish part
