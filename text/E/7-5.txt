Vangelis Pissias Both my uncle and my school teacher wore the Egyptian uniform and joined the Egyptian army as volunteers This was a clear stance to take on the part of the members of the Greek community back then During that war which lasted for several weeks Alexandria was bombed As a child I remember we covered the windows with blue paper to stop the light from getting out I remember one child who used to run through the street shouting Switch off the light so that the planes couldn't see the locations they were targeting
