Kafr Qaddum village North of the West Bank MURAD ESHTEWI POPULAR COMMITTEE OF KAFR QADDUM Kafr Qaddum is a 2 400 hectare Palestinian village of agricultural land The village technically belongs to Qalqilya province Kafr Qaddum's suffering started with the Israeli occupation in 1967 Since then our difficulties have increased The Israelis seized our land and built settlements mainly Kadoumim in 1978 More land was taken by force after that
